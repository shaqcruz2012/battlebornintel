# MIT REAP Stakeholder Lens + Weekly Brief Design

## Goal

Add two features to BBI: (1) a REAP 5-stakeholder filter/lens across Companies, Graph, and Funds views, and (2) a printable Weekly Intelligence Brief view organized by REAP pillars with deal flow metrics for GOED leadership.

## Audience

Same as BBI: SSBCI program managers, economic development officers, GOED leadership.

## Feature 1: REAP Stakeholder Lens

### What

A horizontal chip bar with 5 REAP pillar pills + "All" default, rendered at the top of Companies, Graph, and Funds views. Clicking a pillar filters/highlights entities by stakeholder category.

### Pillar Mappings

| Pillar | Color | Entity Matching Logic |
|--------|-------|-----------------------|
| Risk Capital | Green | Funds array + externals with etype "VC Firm" or "PE Firm" or "Investment Co" |
| Corporations | Blue | Externals with etype "Corporation" |
| Entrepreneurs | Gold | All companies in COMPANIES array |
| Universities | Purple | Ecosystem orgs with etype "University Hub" + externals with etype "University" |
| Government | Red | Ecosystem orgs with etype "Government" or "Economic Development" + externals with etype "Government" |

### Behavior Per View

- **Companies:** Filter companies to those connected (via VERIFIED_EDGES) to entities in the selected pillar. "Entrepreneurs" shows all. "All" shows all.
- **Graph:** Highlight nodes belonging to selected pillar at full opacity; dim all others to 20% opacity. "All" shows normal view.
- **Funds:** Filter funds list. "Risk Capital" shows all funds. Other pillars filter to funds connected to that pillar type. "All" shows all.

### Implementation

- `REAP_PILLARS` constant with id, label, color, icon, and match function
- `reapFilter` useState (default: "all")
- `ReapChipBar` inline component rendered conditionally in Companies, Graph, Funds views
- Entity classification helper function

## Feature 2: Weekly Intelligence Brief

### What

A new "Brief" nav view showing a printable, static weekly snapshot for GOED leadership distribution. Organized by REAP pillars with SSBCI summary and deal flow.

### Layout

**Header:**
- "GOED Weekly Intelligence Brief"
- Week ending date (auto-computed)
- "Battle Born Intelligence" subtitle

**SSBCI Summary Bar:**
- 4 compact KPIs: SSBCI Deployed, Private Capital Leveraged, Portfolio Companies, Avg IRS

**5 REAP Pillar Sections:**
Each pillar card shows:
1. Pillar name + health indicator (entity count)
2. Deal flow highlights (recent timeline events filtered to pillar)
3. Key metrics (pillar-specific)
4. Notable signals

**Footer:**
- "Generated by Battle Born Intelligence" + timestamp
- Print CSS (hide nav, full width)

### Pillar Section Content

- **Risk Capital:** Total deployed, leverage ratio, recent fund activity from timeline
- **Corporations:** Corporate partnerships, acquisitions from timeline/edges
- **Entrepreneurs:** Top movers by momentum, grade distribution, recent funding events
- **Universities:** University hub activity, research partnerships
- **Government:** SSBCI metrics, government grants, regulatory events
